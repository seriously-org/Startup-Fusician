About

It’s hard being an amateur musician - you don’t feel like you’re good enough to perform in front of a live audience or publish your music in a competitive environment, yet you still want to show off your abilities.

Social musical platform "Fusician" provides an easy way for novice musicians to share their music and collaborate with others.


Simple and easy to use

Through an online social-media style platform, users can upload their own work and add new parts and layers to other people's work, allowing them to create unique pieces of music and have fun doing at the same time.

All-in-one solution
